About Us

Sri Krishna Timber Depot, our parent company was started in 1998 by our grand father Shri. Shivji Naran Patel. Our company was started by selling cut-size and timber logs. Within a span of two decades , we have expanded our business to cater residential construction needs by including Doors, Furnitures and other timber related products to our product line-ups.
